Our Social Activities

Anugraha Welfare Society

Anugraha Welfare Society (Anugraha Kalyan Samaj) is a non-profit social organization that was established in 2004 A.D. (2060 B.S.). It is registered in District Administration Office Sunsari (Registration no. 1039/1001) under Society Welfare Council as a social organization. The headquarter is located in Anugraha Church, Dharan-15.

Objective

To contribute in developing a refined and civilized society through the moral and spiritual views with entire development strategy by removing problems present in the society life social deformity, financial jeopardize, moral downfall and its negative effects.

Our Works

Old Age home

An old age home is established which gives shelter to the helpless old people. The building for the house was build with the donation made by the organization's members.
The administrative council manages the work-flow of this house.

Blood Donation Activities

This organization conducts blood donation program anually. It is hold yearly on the 2nd day of "Youth Spiritual Conference" with the aid of "Red Cross Society, Dharan".
The young people are motivated to donate blood as it might save lives of many people as well as benefit the donor's health.
